Coinbase 3 reviews - San Francisco, CA
Securely and compliantly storing crypto assets is core to executing on our vision and building our brand of trust. The Custody team builds and maintains the most secure crypto storage service in the world - storing billions of dollars worth of crypto assets for some of the biggest names in both crypto and finance. We take on hard engineering problems in cryptography, security, blockchain technology and distributed systems.
As the engineering leader for Coinbase Custody, you will build a team at the cutting edge of the crypto ecosystem. You will be tasked with helping Coinbase expand to new assets and services, while owning responsibility for designing, building, and maintaining scalable software systems used to store billions in value.
A little about our team: We take continuous learning to heart - every day in our world brings new ideas and opportunities. We take personal ownership of our mission and believe we will deliver on it. We value positive energy, and clear communication and are committed to building an inclusive environment for people from every background.
Responsibilities
Work with Coinbase Custody’s product lead, engineers, and Coinbase senior leadership to turn our vision into a tangible roadmap every quarter
Build the most reliable, secure and user-friendly Crypto custody platform in the world
Extend Custody to meet the needs of Coinbase and innovative financial institutions around the world
Interface with proprietary cold, warm and hot storage infrastructure to provide a range of Custody use-cases
Provide a powerful, easy to use suite of APIs and documentation for developers to build applications on top of Coinbase Custody
Build internal admin and operations apps to help scale Custody’s client service teams
