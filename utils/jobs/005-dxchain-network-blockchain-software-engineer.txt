DxChain Network - San Francisco, CA
About DxChain
DxChain is a big data and machine learning network powered by a computing-centric blockchain. With a unique chain architecture design and several major innovations, DxChain provides a comprehensive solution for running machine learning algorithms and businesses intelligence on big data and keeping data privacy with security.
Web: http://www.dxchain.com
Responsibilities
As a member of the DxChain engineering team, you will be responsible for:
Write clean, high quality code and test cases, implement the decentralization, distributed blockchain consensus and syncing protocol
Use continuous integration technology to build, deploy and test all aspect of the products to ensure high quality delivery
Strictly adhere to all security protocols and processes and work diligently to protect the assets of the company and its customers
Monitor, research and evaluate Blockchain technologies
Qualifications
Demonstrated hands-on experience with blockchain technology, ideally with Bitcoin, Ethereum and cryptocurrencies in general
Understanding of mainstream consensus algorithm, including but not limited to PoW, PoR, PoS, DPoS, PBFT, Paxos, Raft and so on
Proficient of multiple programming languages such as Golang, Python
Strong knowledge of data structures, algorithms, operating systems, and distributed systems fundamentals.
Understanding of microservices architecture and Docker infrastructure
Understanding of security technologies: PKI, Crypto, identity management
Enthusiasm of working in a high-growth company
A strong technical background with excellent problem solving and multitasking skills
Strong sense of ownership, passion to build quality products for massive scale in collaborative, agile environment and excitement to learn
Job Type: Full-time
