Blockstack is a new internet for decentralized apps. With Blockstack, you own your data and maintain your privacy, security and freedom. Blockstack is open source project and a public benefit corporation. Learn more.
Blockstack is looking for a UI Engineering Partner to help design and implement the Browser portal to decentralized apps. This is a role for a full stack, design focused engineer who can both design and build changes, and who has an eye for both user experience and user interface. This is ideal for engineers who are comfortable working in start-ups, work efficiently, and value high quality design. This person will partner with product managers, and our design and engineering teams to design elegant, clean and simple products.  
Our engineering team builds software using JavaScript/ES6, React, Redux, and Swift on the frontend. 
You will:
- Build Blockstack’s design process for seamless and repeatable structure across product design 
- Design and implement improvements to the Blockstack portal, improving appeal and usability 
- Code and deploy new designs in browser builds 
Qualifications:
You have experience in a rapidly growing start-up creating vision for design brand with cofounders, strong collaboration and communication skills, and can work autonomously to deliver quality results. 5+ years professional experience. 
KPIs:
- Design and build beautiful and functional work for Blockstack
- Engage company wide for consistent design and engineering initiatives 
Skills:
- History of designing web products 
- Can code and build out designs independently with core competencies in HTML, CSS, and Javascript
- Experienced working with multiple stakeholders to implement 
- Nice-to-Have: contributions to open source projects
Qualities + Traits
- Passion for building the new internet for decentralized apps  
- Enthusiasm for hard problems  
- Passion for delivering high-quality designs with quick turnaround times
- Thrives in a fast-paced environment
